## ⚖️ Ethics & Responsible Use

AI text humanizers are powerful tools that should be used responsibly:

- ✅ **Legitimate uses:** Improving writing quality, helping non-native speakers, accessibility
- ❌ **Unethical uses:** Academic dishonesty, plagiarism, deceptive content
- Always disclose AI assistance when required by your institution or publisher
- Respect terms of service of detection tools and academic platforms
- Support responsible AI development and transparency

> See the [Honest Use Guidelines](https://github.com/rudra496/awesome-ai-humanizers/blob/main/ETHICS.md) for more details.
